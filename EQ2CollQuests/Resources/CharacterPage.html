The Character Page

The Character Page is broken up into three main parts: The Character List, The Character's Quest List, The Character's Quest's Items.

The Character List

This area lists your characters in alphabetical order. The buttons at the bottom of the list allow you to add additional characters, update the information about a character from Daybreak Games's Census Server, and delete a character.

To add a character, click on the add button and follow the prompts.

To update a character, highlight the character you wish to have updated and click the update button.

To delete a character, highlight the character you wish to delete, click the delete button and confirm your choice.

Once you have selected a character, that character's name, level and adventuring class will display above the list of collection quests that character is already on.

The Character's Quest List

Clicking on one of the character's quests will show a list of items related to that quest. If the selected character has already collected an item, the check box beside that item will be checked.

At this time, the check boxes cannot be modified. This will change in a future version.
